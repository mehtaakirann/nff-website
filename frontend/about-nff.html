Nagpur Film Festival 2025

Nagpur Film Festival 2025 was organized by the Nagpur Chalchitra Foundation. It provided a platform for young filmmakers to showcase short films based on Indian culture, values, and pressing societal issues.

9th–12th January 2025 | RTMNU
Celebrating Stories that Reflect Bharat
