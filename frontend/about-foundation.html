Who Are We?

Nagpur Chalchitra Foundation (NFF)

Nagpur Chalchitra Foundation (NFF) believes cinema and culture go hand in hand. Cinema mirrors our society, shapes values, and influences change. NFF aims to reflect the true spirit of Bharat — its traditions, values, and vibrant diversity.

While some cinema represents our roots, a significant section distorts Bharatiya ethos. NFF promotes films that unite, not divide — celebrating cultural richness and heritage.

Through training, workshops, internships, festivals, and collaborations, NFF empowers young minds to tell stories of the real Bharat.

Vision

To promote filmmaking, publicity, distribution, and exhibition of films depicting Bharatiyata.

Objectives

  • Promote filmmakers committed to Bharatiya values and ethos
  • Include Bharatness in cinema globally
  • Organize competitive film festivals
  • Identify and train young filmmaking talent
  • Create awareness to appreciate good cinema
